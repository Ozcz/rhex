Goal

Be the first player to lead by 6 points. Points come from where your units stand on the board.

Board

19 hexes in a big hexagon (3-4-5-4-3 rows). Your spawn row = 0 pts. Moving forward: 1, 2, 3 pts. Enemy spawn row = 6 pts.

Controls

Tap a unit to lock its priority order (I, II, III).

Drag a unit to an adjacent hex to assign a move.

Press & hold a unit to activate its skill. Hold again to cancel.

A unit can move or use its skill, not both.

Turns

Setup: Pick 1 skill per unit (duplicates allowed). Press Ready.

Planning: Tap units to lock priority order, drag to move, hold for skill. Press Ready.

Resolution: Both players resolve simultaneously by priority step. Moves beat skills. Equal priority = cancel.

Combat

Move onto an enemy = kill (unless shielded). Dead units respawn on your spawn row next turn.

Skills

  • Shield -- block one attack this turn. Shows an expanding shield effect when activated.
  • Bow -- fire an arrow at a target hex. Arrow travels 1 hex per priority step. Hidden from opponent. Shield blocks arrow.
  • Cloak -- become invisible, but score 0 points while hidden.
