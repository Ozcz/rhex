Goal

Be the first player to lead by 6 points. Points come from where your units stand on the board.

Board

19 hexes in a big hexagon (3-4-5-4-3 rows). Your spawn row = 0 pts. Moving forward: 1, 2, 3 pts. Enemy spawn row = 6 pts.

Controls

Tap a unit to set its priority order (I, II, III). Tap again to move it to top priority.

Drag a unit to an adjacent hex to assign a move.

Press & hold a unit to activate its skill. Hold again to cancel.

A unit can move or use its skill, not both.

Turns

Setup: Pick 1 skill per unit (duplicates allowed). Press Ready.

Planning: Assign actions and set priority order. Tap units to set order, drag to move.

Resolution: Both players resolve simultaneously. Moves beat skills. Equal priority = cancel.

Combat

Move onto an enemy = kill (unless shielded). Dead units respawn on your spawn row next turn.

Skills

  • Shield — block one attack this turn
  • Bow — fire an arrow in a straight line (2 hex range). Hold, then tap direction.
  • Hoe — +1 point while staying on this tile (stacks each turn)
  • Trap — place a hidden trap on an adjacent hex (once per game). Hold, then tap hex.
  • Cloak — become invisible, but score 0 points while hidden
  • Decoy — swap positions with an ally (once per game). Hold, then tap ally.
