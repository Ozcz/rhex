Goal

Be the first player to lead by 6 points. Points come from where your units stand.

Board

19 hexes (3-4-5-4-3 rows). Your spawn = 0 pts. Forward: 1, 2, 3 pts. Enemy spawn = 6 pts.

Controls

Drag a unit to an adjacent hex to move it.

Press & hold a unit to activate its skill. Hold again to cancel.

A unit can move or use its skill, not both.

Turns

Setup: Pick 1 skill per unit. Press Ready.

Planning: Assign actions. The order you assign = priority (1st > 2nd > 3rd).

Resolution: Both players resolve simultaneously. Moves beat skills. Equal priority = cancel.

Combat

Move onto enemy = kill (unless shielded). Dead units respawn next turn.

Skills

  • Shield — Block one attack this turn
  • Bow — Arrow in a straight line (2 hex range). Hold, then tap direction.
  • Hoe — +1 point while on this tile (stacks)
  • Trap — Hidden trap on adjacent hex (once/game). Hold, then tap hex.
  • Cloak — Invisible, score 0 while hidden
  • Decoy — Swap with ally (once/game). Hold, then tap ally.
