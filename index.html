Goal

Be the first player to lead by 6 points. Points come from where your units stand on the board.

Board

19 hexes in a big hexagon (3-4-5-4-3 rows). Your spawn row = 0 pts. Moving forward: 1, 2, 3 pts. Enemy spawn row = 6 pts.

Turns

Setup: Pick 1 skill per unit (duplicates OK). Press Ready when done.

Planning (60s): Assign actions to your 3 units — drag to move or tap skill. The order you assign them sets their priority (1st > 2nd > 3rd).

Resolution: Both players' actions resolve simultaneously. Moves beat skills. Equal priority = cancel.

Combat

Move onto an enemy = kill. Shielded enemies block the kill. Dead units respawn on your spawn row next turn.

Skills

  • Shield — Block one attack this turn
  • Bow — Fire an arrow in a straight line (2 hex range)
  • Hoe — +1 point while staying on this tile (stacks)
  • Trap — Place a hidden trap on an adjacent hex (once per game)
  • Cloak — Go invisible, but score 0 points while hidden
  • Decoy — Swap positions with an ally (once per game)
